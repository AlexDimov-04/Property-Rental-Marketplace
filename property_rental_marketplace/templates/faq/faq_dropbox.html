How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of our homepage and follow the prompts. You'll need to provide some basic information to get started.
How can I search for properties?
Use our search bar to enter keywords, locations, or property types. You can also use filters to narrow down your search based on your preferences.

How do I contact a property owner/agent?
Once you find a property you're interested in, click on it to view the details. You'll find contact information for the property owner or agent on that page.
What should I consider before renting/buying a property?
We recommend considering factors such as location, budget, amenities, and local regulations. It's also a good idea to visit the property in person and ask questions.
How can I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive instructions on how to reset it via email.
Can I save my favorite properties?
Yes, you can create a saved list of properties by creating an account and clicking the "Save" button on property listings.
